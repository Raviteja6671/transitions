Welcome to Ravitejas Niloufer Cafe

Experience authentic flavors and warm hospitality in every cup of our signature Niloufer Tea. Our cafe invites you to relax, refresh, and enjoy delicious snacks inspired by traditional recipes with a contemporary twist.

Our Menu

From the classic Osmania Biscuits to freshly baked cookies, pastries, and a wide variety of teas, we have crafted a menu that delights every palate. Whether you crave savory puffs or sweet treats, every bite tells a story of quality and tradition.

About Us

Established as a beloved neighborhood spot, Ravitejas Niloufer Cafe blends heritage with comfort. Our passion is to serve the perfect tea and snacks that feel like home, accompanied by friendly service in a relaxing ambiance.

Events & Specials

Join us every weekend for special tasting sessions, live acoustic music, and tea brewing workshops. Celebrate the season with our curated menu specials and community gatherings designed to bring tea lovers together.
